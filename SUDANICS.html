    • SUDANICS ETHNIC GROUP IN UGANDA

    Sudanics in Uganda refers to people of South Sudanese or Sudanese descent living in Uganda. Uganda has hosted a significant number of refugees and migrants from South Sudan and Sudan due to conflicts and instability in their countries.

    There are many tribes under Nilotics and these include; Dinka, Madi, Lugbara, Zande and Shilluk among others.

    These tribes are scattered across various refugee settlements and urban centers in Uganda, including:

    1. Bidi Bidi Refugee Settlement
    2. Palorinya Refugee Settlement
    3. Rhino Camp Refugee Settlement
    4. Adjumani Refugee Settlement
    5. Kampala and other urban centers
