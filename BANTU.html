    • BANTU ETHNIC GROUP IN UGANDA

    The Bantu ethnic group is the largest and most diverse ethnic group in Uganda, comprising several sub-groups.The Bantu are believed to have migrated from Cameroon Highlands in early 1000s although the migration took over several centuries.

    There are numerous tribes among the Bantu in Uganda and these inlcude; Baganda, Basoga, Bakiga, Banyankole, Batooro, Bagisu, Bafumbira, Bakonjo and Banyoro among others.

    The Bantu had various characteristics and these include;

    1. The Bantu have a common word "NTU" in their languages
    2. The Bantu are majorly reliant on agriculture as their major occupation
    3. Bantu societies are often organized into clans or extended families.
